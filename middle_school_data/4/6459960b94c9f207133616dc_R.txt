To keep the car's energy, due to the principle of conservation of energy. At the beginning of the ride, potential energy is turned into kinetic energy as the car descends from a height. This kinetic energy continues throughout the ride, ensuring that the car maintains its speed, allowing the car to come to a stop. its important to have excitement and enjoyment to make the overall experience better. 
In summary, It is designed to put the safety of riders first, while having a enjoyable experience from start to finish.
